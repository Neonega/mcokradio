M.C.O.K. Relive!
In case you couldn't catch the DX on 87.2 MHZ
Bad Education Alfie talks too much, doesn’t pay attention and has a crush on Miss Gulliver. Must try harder - especially as he’s the teacher. Classic, silly comedy from Jack Whitehall.

Series 1

1. Parents' Evening

Sitcom. It's parents' evening, and Alfie is under pressure from deputy headmistress Miss Pickwell to mark his mock exam papers, or she will fail his whole class.

2. Sex Education

Parents are worried about the impending arrival of the French exchange students, but this time Fraser is determined to prove that he has got everything under control.

3. Self Defence

Sitcom. A new computer game craze hits Abbey Grove School. Fraser buys a copy to see what all the fuss is about but inadvertently turns his staff into addicts.

4. School Trip

It is the day of the Abbey Grove school field trips and Alfie and Miss Gulliver escort their pupils to a petting zoo and ink museum, but things don't go as planned.

5. Football Match

It is the biggest football match of the year as Abbey Grove take on the local public school. Unfortunately, Alfie has to step in and coach the Abbey Grove team.

6. Politics

School-based sitcom. It is school election time and Gulliver implies Alfie's class are all 'unelectable'. Pickwell has her sights on Fraser's job after he is suspended.

Series 2

1. Swimming Gala

It's the Abbey Grove swimming gala and Alfie's class compete in the synchronised diving....

2. The American

Alfie is shocked when a charming new American teacher takes over his class....

3. Funeral

Abbey Grove is rocked by the news that deputy headmistress Miss Pickwell is dead....

4. Valentine's Day

For Valentine's Day, Mr Fraser plans a special Take Me Out to help shy kids find love....

5. Drugs

Mr Fraser organises a guest speaker for Drugs Awareness Day, to Miss Gulliver's annoyance....

6. Fundraiser

When Abbey Grove is plunged into financial ruin can Alfie come up with a plan to save it?...

Christmas Special

Mr Fraser decides Alfie is the right man to direct the Abbey Grove Christmas play....

Series 3

1. Strike

Alfie must stand by Miss Gulliver when she leads the teachers on strike.

2. After School Clubs

Alfie tries to attend a book group, a LARPing club and a leaving party at the same time.

3. Sports Day

It's sports day and Fraser has hired the most inappropriate new teacher to oversee events.

4. Fundraising

After running him over, Alfie and his class raise funds for the caretaker's surgery.

5. The Exam

Alfie never passed his biology GCSE, so he must resit the exam with his own class.

6. The Finale

Alfie realises he can't go on teaching without his beloved class and quits Abbey Grove.
