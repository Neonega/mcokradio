Carradine’s Cockney Sing-a-long: 3rd November, 2021
Born out of the late night, cider fuelled, sing-a-longs in the Nag's Head bar at Twinwood Vintage Music Festival in 2013 and 2014, Carradine's Cockney Sing-a-long was launched in the Mahogany Bar at Wilton's Music Hall in October 2014. Tom Carradine, pianist with Champagne Charlie and the Bubbly Boys and regular accompanist on the London cabaret circuit, decided that he wanted to bring the atmosphere and joyous community singing of the Twinwood sing-a-longs to London, and so created this monthy event. www.carradinescockneysingalong.co.uk Download additional materials for this episode: x1 Songbook
