11. Dog
Charlie and his family move from London to run a bed and breakfast by the sea. Ben is given a dog as a present, and Charlie and Alison persuade him to sneak his new pet into a local arcade. But, when Charlie is spotted in sunglasses and with the dog, he is mistaken for being blind. He goes along with the misunderstanding - but this spirals out of control until the whole of Scarborough is involved in a charity fun run to buy him a new guide dog.
