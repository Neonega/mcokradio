1. The Bone Plot
When a historical documentary team comes to film at Button House, Alison and the ghosts are shocked to discover that the assassination plot they are investigating involved one of their own. Meanwhile, Mike - always on the lookout for free publicity - begs Alison to accept the offer of a television interview about the old house. The only hitch is that Alison is terrified of speaking on camera. Luckily, Thomas is there to coach her in the art of speechmaking. What could possibly go wrong?
